# Interplanetary Travel

A space explorer travels from the planet _World of Code_ to _Code World_, and from there proceeds to _Codice Mondiale_.

He wants to know the total distance traveled in these two legs of the journey to record in his logbook.

## Input
The input consists of two integers $A$ and $B$, representing the distances of the two legs of the trip.
*Domain: $-10^4 \leq A, B \leq 10^4$*

## Output
Print a single integer representing the total distance traveled.

## Examples
| Input | Output |
| :--- | :--- |
| 10<br>20 | 30 |

| Input | Output |
| :--- | :--- |
| 5<br>3 | 8 |
