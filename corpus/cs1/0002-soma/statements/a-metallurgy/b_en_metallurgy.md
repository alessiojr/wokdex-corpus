# Metal Alloy

In metallurgy, we often combine two metals to form a new alloy with superior properties.

You are developing software to control the foundry scale. Given the weights of two blocks of metal that will be melted together, your program must calculate the total weight of the resulting alloy.

## Input
The input contains two integers $A$ and $B$, one on each line, representing the weight of the two metals to be fused.

*Constraints:* $-10^4 \leq A, B \leq 10^4$

## Output
Print a single integer representing the total weight of the alloy (the sum of the two weights).

## Examples
| Input | Output |
| :--- | :--- |
| 10<br>20 | 30 |

| Input | Output |
| :--- | :--- |
| 5<br>3 | 8 |
