# The Fusion

Two distinct elements enter the crucible of existence.
They lose their individuality to the heat.
They become one.

What was separate is now united.
Mass is conserved.
Form alters.

Receive the parts.
Reveal the whole.

## Input
Two integer values.
($-10^4$ to $10^4$)

## Output
The result of the union.

## Examples
| Input | Output |
| :--- | :--- |
| 3<br>4 | 7 |
